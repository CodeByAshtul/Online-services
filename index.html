Our Services

  • Bank Account

    Open a new bank account online with ease. No need to visit the bank branch.

  • Aadhaar Seva Kendra

    Apply for Aadhaar card, update your details or get duplicate card.

  • Shop Act

    Get registered under the Shops and Establishment Act online. Apply for a new registration or renewal.

  • Udyam Aadhaar

    Register your MSME business with Udyam Aadhaar. Get your MSME certificate online.

  • Food License

    Get a Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) license online. Apply for a new license or renewal.

  • Driving License

    Apply for a driving license online. Book an appointment for the driving test and download the license once you pass.

  • Farmers' Scheme Application

    Apply for various farmers' schemes offered by the government. Check eligibility and apply online.

  • Worker Registration

    Register as a worker in various government schemes online. Check eligibility and apply online.

  • Gazette Notification

    Get your name published in the Gazette of India. Apply for a new publication or make corrections to an existing one.

  • Voter Registration

    Apply for voter registration online. Check your application status and download the voter ID card once it's ready.

  • Mahamandal LOI

    Apply for the Letter of Intent (LOI) for the Maharashtra State Co-operative Housing Society Federation Limited (Mahamandal).

  • All PF-related work

    Complete all Provident Fund (PF) related work online. Check your PF balance, transfer your PF account, and more.

  • Birth Certificate/Death Certificate

    Apply for a birth or death certificate online. Track your application status and download the certificate once it's ready.

  • Urgent PAN Card

    Apply for a PAN card on an urgent basis. Get your PAN card delivered within a few days.

  • Ayushman Bharat Card

    Apply for an Ayushman Bharat Card online. Get access to healthcare services at empaneled hospitals.

  • E-Shram Card

    Apply for an E-Shram Card online. This card provides various benefits such as health and insurance schemes, pension, and more.

  • MSEB New Connection Application

    Apply for a new electricity connection with MSEB online. Track your application status and get updates on the installation process.
